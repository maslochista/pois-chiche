Hey Pois Chiche!

We are a brand created in Berlin that started of as an inside joke between two friends. A French guy and a girl not speaking French apart from the word Pois Chiche - which is Chickpeas in French.

Our mission is to make you feel comfortable and include as many people as we can in our inside joke, of being a Pois-Chiche, the most powerful of beans.

Feel comfy, a bit goofy, wear it to be confident and create a positive vibe wherever you go.

It's also a nice ice breaker ;)
